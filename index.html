Architecture Design Process to Construction

The studio workflow moves from requirement study and concept planning through technical development, coordination, and execution support on site.

Step 01 Client Brief and Site Understanding

We begin with lifestyle needs, plot conditions, climate, circulation requirements, and the budget framework before design directions are proposed.

Step 02 Concept Planning and 3D Development

Initial plans, facade options, and 3D views are developed to test massing, room relationships, light, and the overall architectural identity.

Step 03 Technical Drawings and Coordination

After approval, the design is translated into working drawings, floor plans, elevations, sections, and coordinated interior or structural inputs.

Step 04 Construction and Site Execution

During construction, the studio supports execution with clarifications, design checks, and decisions that help retain the quality of the approved concept.
